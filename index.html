Early Completion Benefit

If any phase is completed ahead of schedule, the corresponding payment will be due upon completion of that phase, regardless of the originally estimated timeframe. This allows us to maintain project momentum and potentially deliver the entire solution earlier than projected.

Early completion of a phase triggers the start of the next phase immediately, potentially reducing the overall project timeline.
